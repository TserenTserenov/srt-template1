# Структура встречи с venue owner

## Назначение документа

Детальная структура встречи с владельцем venue для получения трёх конкретных договоренностей (commitment) о партнёрстве.

## Три целевых результата встречи

1. **✅ Согласие быть в приложении** — venue owner подтверждает готовность быть представленным в приложении
2. **✅ Коллаборационные публикации** — договоренность о совместном контенте в соцсетях
3. **✅ Доступ для съёмок** — venue предоставляет пространство для авторских съёмок (не только коллабы)

---

## Подготовка к встрече

### За день до встречи

- [ ] Отправить подтверждение встречи (WhatsApp/Email)
- [ ] Подготовить презентационные материалы на телефоне/планшете
- [ ] Изучить venue детально (меню, Instagram, отзывы, целевая аудитория)
- [ ] Подготовить конкретные идеи для их venue
- [ ] Распечатать/подготовить agreement (если нужно)

### Материалы с собой

1. **Портфолио** — 5-7 лучших примеров контента (видео, фото)
2. **Кейсы** — примеры коллабораций с другими venues (если есть)
3. **Аналитика** — статистика аудитории, engagement, reach
4. **Mockup приложения** — скриншоты/прототип, как venue будет выглядеть в app
5. **Идеи для их venue** — 2-3 конкретные идеи для контента
6. **Контакты для связи** — визитки, QR-код

---

## Agenda встречи (15-20 минут)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Минута 0-2:   Small talk, установление rapport             │
│ Минута 2-7:   Презентация проекта и ценности               │
│ Минута 7-12:  Обсуждение трёх форматов партнёрства         │
│ Минута 12-15: Конкретные CTA и получение commitment        │
│ Минута 15-18: Обсуждение деталей и next steps              │
│ Минута 18-20: Фиксация договоренностей, follow-up          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Часть 1: Установление rapport (2 минуты)

### Small talk

```
"Thanks so much for taking the time to meet!
I really love this place — [конкретный комплимент о venue:
интерьер, атмосфера, меню, etc.]"

[Если есть общий контекст:]
"I've actually been here a few times — [конкретная деталь]."
```

### Переход к делу

```
"So, I'll keep this quick and focused. I know your time is valuable.

Today I want to show you:
1. What we're building
2. How featuring your venue benefits you
3. Three specific ways we can collaborate

Sound good?"
```

## Часть 2: Презентация проекта (5 минут)

### Кто мы

```
"We're building a platform for students in Enschede — especially
from University of Twente — to discover the best places in the city.

But we're not just listing venues. We're creating engaging,
high-quality content that actually gets people excited to visit."
```

### Что мы делаем

```
"Here's what makes us different:

[Показать примеры на телефоне]

1. We create professional short-form videos and photos —
   TikTok, Instagram Reels, Stories

2. We focus on storytelling, not just advertising —
   we show the vibe, the experience, the unique aspects of each place

3. Our content gets thousands of views from local students
   who are actively looking for places to hang out

[Показать аналитику, если есть]

This is our reach so far — and we're just getting started."
```

### Целевая аудитория

```
"Our audience is:
- Students from University of Twente (15,000+ students)
- International and local students
- Age 18-28, active on social media
- Looking for cafes, bars, study spots, hangout places

This is exactly the demographic that visits places like yours."
```

## Часть 3: Три формата партнёрства (5 минут)

### Формат 1: Venue в приложении

**Презентация:**

```
"First, we want to feature your venue in our app.

[Показать mockup]

Here's how it would look:
- Your name, photos, description
- Location, hours, menu/offerings
- Direct link to your Instagram and website
- Reviews and ratings from students

It's completely free, and it gives you visibility
to thousands of students using the app."
```

**CTA #1:**

```
"Are you comfortable with us featuring [название venue]
in the app this way?"
```

**Обработка возражений:**

| Возражение | Ответ |
|------------|-------|
| "What information do you need?" | "Just basic info — name, address, hours, a few photos, and your Instagram. We can pull most of it from your website." |
| "Can we review it first?" | "Absolutely! We'll send you a preview before it goes live, and you can request any changes." |
| "How do we update it later?" | "You'll have access to update your profile anytime, or you can just message us." |

---

### Формат 2: Коллаборационные публикации

**Презентация:**

```
"Second, we'd love to create collaborative content with you.

Here's how it works:
- We shoot professional content at your venue (photos, videos)
- We feature your space, your products, your vibe
- We post it on our channels AND yours
- We tag each other, cross-promote

[Показать примеры коллабораций]

For example, we could create a video showcasing your best drinks,
or a 'day in the life' style reel, or a student favorites series.

You get high-quality content for your socials for free,
and we get to feature your venue to our audience."
```

**CTA #2:**

```
"Would you be open to doing 1-2 collaborative posts per month?
We handle all the production — you just approve the content
before we post."
```

**Обработка возражений:**

| Возражение | Ответ |
|------------|-------|
| "We already have someone doing our content" | "That's great! This would be additional content, not replacing what you're doing. Plus, it reaches a different audience — our followers." |
| "We don't have time to coordinate" | "No problem — we make it super easy. We come, shoot, edit, and send you the final version for approval. Takes maybe 30 minutes of your time total." |
| "What if we don't like the content?" | "You have full approval before anything goes live. If you don't like it, we don't post it. Simple as that." |

---

### Формат 3: Доступ для авторских съёмок

**Презентация:**

```
"Third — and this is the most valuable part for us —
we'd love to occasionally use your space for our creative shoots.

This isn't just about featuring your venue.
We're building a content engine to grow our brand,
and we need beautiful, interesting locations for our videos.

Here's what this looks like:
- We come in during off-peak hours (when you're not busy)
- We shoot our own creative content — vlogs, skits, lifestyle videos
- We always credit and tag your venue in the posts
- It's free promotion for you — thousands of people see your space

[Показать примеры авторского контента]

For example, we might shoot a 'student life in Enschede' video here,
or a 'best study spots' series, or lifestyle content with our team.

You get visibility, we get access to a great location."
```

**CTA #3:**

```
"Would you be open to letting us shoot here occasionally —
maybe 2-3 times per month during quiet hours?

We'll always coordinate with you in advance,
and we'll make sure it doesn't disrupt your business."
```

**Обработка возражений:**

| Возражение | Ответ |
|------------|-------|
| "We can't have filming during business hours" | "Totally understand. We can come early morning, late afternoon, or whenever works for you. We're flexible." |
| "What if it's disruptive?" | "We're a small crew — usually just 1-2 people with a phone or camera. Super low-key, won't bother customers." |
| "Do we need to provide anything?" | "Nope! We bring everything. You just give us access. We handle the rest." |
| "How do we know you'll tag us?" | "We'll send you every post before or after it goes live, and you'll see the tags. Plus, it's in our interest to tag you — it builds credibility." |

## Часть 4: Фиксация договоренностей (3 минуты)

### Checklist согласия

```
"Awesome! So just to confirm, here's what we agreed on:

✅ [Venue name] will be featured in our app
✅ We'll create 1-2 collaborative posts per month together
✅ We can occasionally shoot our creative content here during quiet hours

Does that sound right?"
```

### Документирование

**Вариант 1: Устная договоренность**

```
"Perfect! I'll send you a summary of everything we discussed
via WhatsApp/email today, and we can start as soon as you're ready."
```

**Вариант 2: Письменное соглашение (если venue owner хочет формальности)**

```
"Would you like me to send over a simple partnership agreement
that outlines this? Nothing complicated — just a one-pager
to make sure we're both clear on expectations."
```

## Часть 5: Next steps и follow-up (2 минуты)

### Конкретные шаги

```
"Here's what happens next:

1. Today/tomorrow:
   - I'll send you examples and a summary of our chat

2. This week:
   - We'll schedule our first collaborative shoot
   - I'll send you the info we need for the app listing

3. Next week:
   - First content goes live
   - We start promoting your venue

Sound good?"
```

### Первая съёмка — назначить сразу

```
"Actually, when would be a good time for our first shoot?
We could come in next [день недели] around [время] if that works?"
```

## Часть 6: Closing

```
"Thanks so much for your time and for being open to this!
I think this is going to be a great partnership.

[Обмен контактами, если еще не сделали]

I'll follow up today with all the details.
Looking forward to working together!"
```

## Ключевые принципы встречи

1. **Краткость** — не более 20 минут, уважай время owner
2. **Ценность сначала** — покажи, что они получают, не только что ты хочешь
3. **Конкретика** — не абстрактные идеи, а конкретные форматы и примеры
4. **Гибкость** — адаптируй под их нужды и возражения
5. **Фиксация** — всегда письменное подтверждение после встречи
6. **Быстрый старт** — назначь первую съёмку сразу, не откладывай
